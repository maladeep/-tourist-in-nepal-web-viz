The page presents the visualization of tourist arrivals in Nepal from 2009 - 2014

Tourism is the largest industry in Nepal and its largest source of foreign exchange and revenue. Possessing eight of the ten highest mountains globally, Nepal is a hot spot destination for mountaineers, rock climbers, and people seeking adventure. The Hindu and Buddhist heritage of Nepal and its cold weather are also strong attractions.

The tourist industry is seen as a way to alleviate poverty and achieve more significant social equity. Tourism brings $471 million a year to Nepal.
